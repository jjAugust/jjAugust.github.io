Biography

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in the Computer Science and Engineering department at University of South Florida (USF). I received a M.S. degree from Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications in 2019 and a B.Eng. degree in Computer Science from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications in 2012. I also have 4 years of experience working in industry. Current research focuses on cybersecurity under the supervision of Dr. Yao Liu.
